About Butter Lassi / Laban

Our Butter Lassi, traditionally known as Laban, is a delightful blend of creamy yogurt, rich butter, and a hint of salt. This traditional Middle Eastern drink is not only refreshing but also packed with nutritional benefits.

Ingredients

Fresh cow's milk yogurt, pure butter, salt, and water. Contains no artificial preservatives, colors, or flavors.

Benefits

Butter Lassi is rich in probiotics that promote gut health, calcium for strong bones, and provides a good source of energy. It's perfect for hot days or as a digestive aid after meals.

How to Enjoy

Serve chilled as a refreshing drink, pair with meals, or use as a base for smoothies. Shake well before serving.
